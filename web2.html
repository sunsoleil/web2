Plot

Louisa Clark is a small town girl who knows a lot about the people around her.
Will is a quadriplegic man who hates everyone around him.
Louisa is hired by Will's mum to take care of him for six months.
Although they have a rocky start, their relationship grows over time, going for hate, to like, to love.
